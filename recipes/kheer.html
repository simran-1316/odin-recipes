Kheer Recipe

Description

A sweet dish made with love.

Ingrediants

  • Uncooked Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Dry Fruits

Steps

  1. Add half litre milk in a container
  2. Add Sugar and Boil it for 10 minutes
  3. Add rice and cook it for 20 minutes
  4. Now take the dish and add dry fruits for finishing
  5. Your Kheer is ready to eat!!
