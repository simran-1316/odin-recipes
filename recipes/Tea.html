Tea Recipe

Description

A classic Indian tea is all you need to freshen up your mind.

Ingrediants

  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of water in a pan
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves
  3. Add sugar according to your taste
  4. Now add a little piece of mashed ginger
  5. Let it boil
  6. Now add 1 cup of milk
  7. Let it boil for 5 minutes
  8. Your chai is ready!!
