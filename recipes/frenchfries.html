French Fries

Description

A crispy and salty dish made with potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Peri Peri Masala
  • Cooking Oil
  • Corn Flour

Steps

  1. Peal and cut the potatoes in rectangular shape
  2. Boil them in a container and also add salt
  3. Boil the potatoes for 10 minutes
  4. After 10 minutes take out the potatoes and let them dry for 10 minutes
  5. Now add and heat the cooking oil in another container on high flame
  6. Add corn flour with potatoes sticks and coat them
  7. Then fry the potatoes stick two times on high flame
  8. Now toss them with peri peri masala
  9. Your french fries are ready. Enjoy them with any sauce!!
